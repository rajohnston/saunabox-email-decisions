A seat is more than email

We're building HQ and want the team to have the full features. Anyone can log in with any email, but the deeper features only work for people on the @saunabox.co.uk domain. If we move someone to a personal address to save the seat, here is what they lose.

The calendar is the point. Set-availability, the website "book Tom/Ryan", clash detection and email logging only work for people on the domain. Off-domain, calendar drops to busy-only and email can't be logged. So we keep active HQ users on the domain even if they barely send email, and only alias people who aren't HQ users and only receive mail.
